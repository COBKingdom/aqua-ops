# AquaOps 💧

AquaOps is a simple and powerful mobile-first app designed to help water factory owners manage their daily operations efficiently.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 📦 Production Tracking (Sachet & Bottled Water)
- 💰 Sales Management
- 💸 Expense Tracking
- 📊 Profit Calculation
- 💵 Cash Tracking (Real money received)
- 📉 Debt Management (Customer balances)
- 📱 Mobile App (Installable on phone)

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

| Metric | Meaning     |
|--------|------------|
| Profit | Performance |
| Cash   | Real money  |
| Debt   | Risk        |

---

## 🧠 Why AquaOps?

Many water factory owners struggle to track:

- Daily production
- Sales vs actual cash
- Customer debts
- Real profit

AquaOps solves this with a simple and intuitive interface designed for real-world use.

---

## 📱 Built For

- Sachet water factories (Pure water)
- Bottled water producers
- Small to medium scale operations

---

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

- Next.js
- Supabase (Database)
- Tailwind CSS
- Vercel (Deployment)
